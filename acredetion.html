More About INTESOL Worldwide

INTESOL Worldwide was founded in the UK in 1993 in the belief that anyone possessing the passion to teach, the skills to express themselves clearly, and the desire to make a difference in the lives of others should be given the opportunity and encouragement to gain a professional certification in TESOL.

Today, INTESOL has accumulated over 26 years of quality course provision, and a proven track record as a renowned and respected accredited TEFL/TESOL course provider.

Westminster College London’s role is to help each teacher come into confident possession of his/her innate talents, improve the skills needed for success in Teaching English as a Foreign Language, and establish values that will allow him/her to act with thoughtfulness. Westminster College London (WCL) was established in 2017 and provided TEFL and Education courses just in the UK. WCL expanded the TEFL courses in Europe, Middle East, Asia, America, and Africa and now has more than 40 centers across the globe. Students can benefit from the online TEFL and classroom based courses which are unique in design. WCL is a Focus Awards approved centre entitled to offer its Ofqual Regulated Qualifications which are internationally recognised. WCL non-regulated courses are accredited by the CPD Group, accreditation with the CPD Group provides an assurance that we have satisfied a set of academic, ethical and operational standards.
